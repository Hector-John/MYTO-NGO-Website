About Us

Milango Ya Tumaini Organization (MYTO) is an inter-independent registered non-governmental organization. The Phrase “Milango Ya Tumaini” is Kiswahili word meaning “Door of Hope” It is a non-profit making organization. The Milango Ya Tumaini is registered with Government of Kenya at the office of the President under the Ministry of Interior & Co-Ordination of the National Government NGOs and Co-Ordination Board Act with the Registration No OP.218/051/20-167/11924 Introduction and Background. . It is based at the Kenyan Coast, Mombasa County.

Our Mission

Milango Ya Tumaini Organization is dedicated to eradicate poverty through Empowerment on Education, Economic and Health Care.

Motto

MYTO’S prime motto is Community Empowerment and Capacity Building to the deprived and marginalized people of the society.
To alleviate poverty, improve access to education, health care services, mitigating the impact of HIV/AIDs among orphans, vulnerable children and the poor in general.

Our Vision

To care, serve, give hope and change livelihood.

How we work

Influenced by the new development paradigm, which puts people before things, MYTO follows a maximum participatory team approach in every sphere of its work. The Community knowledge and culture is valued and respected, and there is confidence that the community are capable of self-reliant. On a practical level, the fundamental thrust of this approach is decentralization and empowerment.
